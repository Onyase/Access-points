Uganda E-Learning Initiative for Education Institutions - Uganda Rural Development and Training Programmme (URDT)- Access Points

This interactive map provides a visual representation of key access points for the Uganda E-Learning Initiative in Uganda Rural Development and Training Programmme (URDT). Each icon on the map represents a location where educational infrastructure is being integrated to support digital learning. These locations play a vital role in enhancing access to educational resources and improving learning outcomes for students and staff in Uganda.
